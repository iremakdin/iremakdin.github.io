Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. In which languages do you provide counseling services?

I conduct counseling sessions in Turkish, my native language, and English, in which I have advanced proficiency (C2 level).

2. What methods do you work with?

In my practice, I primarily adopt the Humanistic approach. Additionally, I integrate the Somatic Experiencing method, which focuses on the nervous system and body awareness, into my sessions to offer a holistic perspective.

3. Where do sessions take place? Is online counseling possible?

Yes, I offer both in-person and online services.

  • In-Person: I conduct sessions in Basel (in collaboration with Praxis Evia).
  • Online: I also provide remote video counseling services based on your needs.

5. In which areas do you provide support?

Within the scope of individual counseling for adults, my main areas of practice include relationship issues, the impact of childhood experiences on the present, emotion regulation difficulties, and life adjustment processes (such as relocation).

6. Does health insurance cover session fees?

No, my services are not covered by health insurance (basic or supplementary insurance). Currently, I can only accept self-paying clients.

7. What is the session fee?

The standard fee for individual sessions is CHF 140 for 60 minutes. This time includes the direct consultation period and necessary preparation and follow-up work.
